English III - Final Work

Complete the following sections as instructed below.

General Structure

The final work consists of the following parts:

  1. Make a shopping list: Create a list of food items with images.
  2. Write a past event paragraph: Share a significant story from your life using past tense.
  3. Use comparatives: Describe and compare places or objects using comparative adjectives.

Ensure each section is clear, visually appealing, and follows the instructions carefully.

Estructura del trabajo final

1. Make a Shopping List

Create a shopping list with food products. Follow these steps:

  • Include at least 10 food items.
  • Attach images for each product.
  • Present the list in a neat and organized manner.

2. Write two or more Paragraph About a Past Event

Write about something important that has happened in your life. Guidelines:

  • Use the past tense consistently in your paragraph.
  • Include at least two images representing the event.
  • Write a clear and engaging paragraph with proper grammar and structure.

3. Describe Places and Things Using Comparatives

Compare two places or objects using comparative adjectives. Instructions:

  • Select two places or two objects to compare.
  • Write at least five sentences using comparative adjectives (e.g., taller, more beautiful).
  • Attach images of the places or objects.
