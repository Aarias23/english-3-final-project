Work Structure

Review the required structure for your final work.

Structure and Formatting

  • Presentation: Include a cover page with the title of your work.
  • Index: List the sections and subsections of your work.
  • Introduction:
    • Explain the purpose and main goal of your work.
  • Justification: Provide a short justification on the importance of the topic.
  • Development: Write the main content in English.
  • Conclusions: Summarize the main findings of your work.
  • Bibliography: List all references in the required format.
  • Annexes: Include any supporting materials, such as images or data.

Formatting Guidelines

  • Save the document as PDF.
  • Title: Font size 14, Arial Black.
  • Text: Font size 12, Arial, 1.5 line spacing.
  • Page number: Bottom left.
  • Header: Title of the work on the right.
  • Footer: Name of the student on the right.
